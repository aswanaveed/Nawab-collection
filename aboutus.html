About Us

How It Began

Nawab Collection traces its roots back over three decades to a bedding venture established by MR.Ghulam Ali in Faisalabad, Pakistan’s textile hub. Built on quality and craftsmanship, the business steadily grew its presence and reputation within the local market.

Over time, it developed in-house design and printing capabilities, offering a range of premium bedding in diverse materials and finishes. Today, Nawab Collection stands as a well-established Faisalabad-based enterprise, recognized for reliability, design excellence, and enduring comfort.

NETWORK
